Global Migration, 1960–2022

A representation of annual migration estimates, as viewed by the Earth. This provides a refreshing, uplifting viewpoint on a subject usually looked down upon.
Legend:
Similar colors indicate high levels of migration between the two nations. Population in a given year is represented by the size of the nodes, likewise for migration counts and the mobile blobs. Much of this data is imputed.
Source:
Standaert, Samuel; Rayp, Glenn (2025), "Annual Bilateral Migration Data - 1960-2022", Mendeley Data, V2, doi: 10.17632/cpt3nh6jct.2
